Welcome to CodeRetreat Frankfurt

This is the website for the CodeRetreat event taking place in Frankfurt (the Rhein-Main one) on Dec 3rd 2011.
It's part of the Global Day of CodeRetreat, where over thousand developers around the world spend the day practicing the craft of software development and share their passion for Software Craftsmanship.

Want to know what a CodeRetreat is? Check out our explanation. Or just go ahead and register your spot, and be a part of this exciting day and save your chance to win some special prices throughout the day.

Sponsors

This event is sponsored by andrena objects and mediaman.

What exactly is CodeRetreat Frankfurt?

CodeRetreat is an event where developers come together to practice the craft of software development. We want to learn and share the basics of good software development.

A more general description (incl. a bit of history and a video of a CodeRetreat in Cleveland) can be found on our separate "What is a CodeRetreat?" page.

How does it work?:

  • CodeRetreat Frankfurt is a day long event taking place on Dec 3rd 2011
  • We work in pairs.
  • We use Test-Driven Development.
  • We start at 8am in the morning and finish around 5.30pm in the afternoon.
  • Breakfast and a lunch is provided by our sponsors.
  • Any developer can participate. You can use any language and environment.
  • The day is split into six sessions.
  • Each session lasts 45 minutes and is spent solving Conway’s Game of Life.
  • After each session we delete our code, hold a short retrospective and switch pairs.
  • After the event we'll go to the pub together.

Requirements:

  • You should know about unit-testing. What is it? How do I write a unit-test?
  • You should bring your laptop with your preferred development environment installed and setup for writing unit-tests.

Special Prices?

Where?

The event will take place at the Brotfabrik in Frankfurt/Main (Bachmannstr. 2-4). It's the same venue that other events like the Webmontag Frankfurt, Java User Group Frankfurt take place.
