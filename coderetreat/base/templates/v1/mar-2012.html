CodeRetreat Frankfurt / Rhein-Main

CodeRetreat is an event where developers come together to practice the craft of software development. We want to learn and share the basics of good software development.

CodeRetreats are about allowing yourself to take a detour from your daily programmers' life. Make time and room and give your mind space to practice known techniques and learn new ones. Together with a group of enthusiastic developers you will encounter new approaches to solving a seemingly old problem. In an environment that's tailored towards learning and no deadlines, you will pick up new skills with ease and rediscover your passion for programming

Give yourself time to evolve. Become a better software craftsman.
